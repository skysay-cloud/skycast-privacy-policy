Privacy Policy for SkyCast

Effective Date: May 18, 2025

SkyCast does not collect personally identifiable information from users.

Advertising

SkyCast uses Google AdMob to serve ads in the free version. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data as outlined in their privacy policies.

Location Data

The app uses location services only to provide local weather forecasts and cloud maps. Your location is never stored or shared by us.

In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase the “Remove Ads” option, Google Play handles all payment processing securely. We do not store payment details.

User Accounts

No user registration or login is required to use the app.
